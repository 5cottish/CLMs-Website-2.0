Our Programs

Discover how we are transforming lives through intentional, Christ‑centered initiatives

Evangelism

We share the good news of Jesus Christ through campus outreaches, crusades, and one‑on‑one discipleship. Our goal is to see every university student in Uganda have an opportunity to respond to the gospel.

  • Campus Crusades: Large‑scale evangelistic events on university campuses.
  • One‑on‑One Discipleship: Personal mentorship and spiritual guidance.
  • Outreach Missions: Taking the gospel to unreached communities around campuses.

Discipleship

We nurture students to grow in faith through Bible studies, mentorship, and leadership training. Our discipleship pathway equips believers to become mature followers of Christ and future leaders in church and society.

  • Bible Study Groups: Weekly gatherings for Scripture study and fellowship.
  • Leadership Training: Workshops and seminars on servant leadership.
  • Mentorship Program: Connecting students with experienced Christian mentors.

Compassionate Empathy

We meet practical needs through community outreach, support programs, and holistic care. We believe faith without works is dead, and we demonstrate God's love in tangible ways.

  • Community Outreach: Serving vulnerable communities in Kampala and beyond.
  • Student Support: Providing meals, school supplies, and emotional support.
  • Health Initiatives: Health camps and wellness programs.

Partner With Us

Your support enables us to expand these programs and reach even more students across Uganda.
