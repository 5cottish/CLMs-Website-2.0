About Christian Legacy Makers

Discover our story, mission, and the values that drive us

Who We Are

Christian Legacy Makers (CLM) is an evangelical interdenominational Christian organization committed to sharing the good news of Jesus Christ on university and college campuses in Uganda; nurturing students to grow and become effective witnesses of Christ and leaders in the church and society, through Evangelism, Discipleship, and Compassionate Empathy.

Founded in 2014, CLM has grown from a small group of passionate believers to a nationwide movement impacting thousands of students across Uganda. We believe that by investing in the spiritual formation of university students today, we are shaping the leaders, pastors, and change-makers of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To share the good news of Jesus Christ on university and college campuses in Uganda; nurturing students to grow and become effective witnesses of Christ and leaders in the church and society, through Evangelism, Discipleship, and Compassionate Empathy.

Our Vision

A generation of Godly leaders transformed by the gospel, impacting every sphere of society for the glory of God.

Our Values

Faithfulness, Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Unity, and Servant Leadership.
